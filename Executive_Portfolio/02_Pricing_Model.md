## Revenue Model Overview

HAVEN operates on a **B2G (Business-to-Government) SaaS subscription model**. Government agencies and social service nonprofits pay a monthly subscription. Residents use the platform entirely free — funded by government contracts.

```
Government / Agency  ──pays──►  HAVEN  ──free──►  Residents
```

## End-User Pricing

**$0 — Always free for every user.**

All user-facing features are fully subsidized by the government/agency subscription. Users never pay, never see ads, never have data sold. HAVEN serves any person navigating government regardless of income, status, or circumstance.

### Who Deploys HAVEN (Paying Customers)
- County social services and human services departments
- Municipal government portals and 311 systems
- State agencies (DMV, EDD, DHSS, DPH, courts)
- Veterans affairs offices and VA-affiliated nonprofits
- Housing authorities (emergency, interim, Section 8)
- Workforce development boards and job centers
- Nonprofit networks, legal aid organizations, community benefit orgs
- Immigration legal services and naturalization support programs
- Any government-adjacent organization guiding people through civic systems
