## What HAVEN Is

HAVEN is a universal civic navigation platform built for **everyone** — not limited to any income bracket, housing status, or demographic. Any person who needs to interact with government at any level, for any reason, is HAVEN's user.

HAVEN simplifies, guides, and automates the full spectrum of government interaction:

- **Justice System** — court filings, eviction defense, legal aid, public defender, reentry
- **Transportation** — DMV renewals, new registration, title transfers, public transit info, paratransit
- **Benefits & Aid** — SNAP/CalFresh, SSI, TANF/CalWORKs, WIC, General Assistance
- **Employment** — Unemployment insurance (EDD), workforce training, job placement
- **Housing** — Emergency shelter, interim housing, Section 8 vouchers, general housing navigation
- **Healthcare** — Medi-Cal, Medicare, VITA, mental health, substance use, free clinics
- **IRS & Tax** — VITA free filing, tax credits, amended returns
- **Veterans Services** — VA disability, HUD-VASH, pension, healthcare
- **Domestic Violence** — Safety planning, shelter, restraining orders, DV agency routing
- **Immigration** — Legal services, DACA, naturalization navigation
- **Seniors & Youth** — Meals on Wheels, IHSS, youth drop-in, childcare
- **Utilities** — LIHEAP, PG&E CARE, water assistance
- **Every other area** where government intersects with daily life

BB — HAVEN's digital multi-language AI Caseworker — guides or automates every one of these interactions seamlessly, in plain language, across every domain.

**Data Processing**: Collection, transmission, storage, and processing of highly sensitive PII including SSNs, DOBs, tax forms, income brackets, and medical routing data — all protected via Apex Vault (AES-256-GCM field-level encryption).
