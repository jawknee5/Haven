## Executive Summary

HAVEN delivers a **~230x return on investment** for county governments in Year 1. For every $1 spent on the HAVEN platform, a county saves or generates $230 in staff efficiency, avoided crisis costs, and newly accessed resident services — across **all** government domains, not just social services.

HAVEN is not a single-purpose tool. Every government interaction that currently requires a citizen to physically visit an office, navigate a confusing website, or wait on hold becomes a HAVEN-automated workflow. The savings below represent only the social services slice. The full ROI — including DMV, court, transit, tax, and employment automation — is materially larger.

## Case Study: Santa Clara County
*Population: 1.9M | Social services eligible: ~60,000 residents*

### Current State — Without HAVEN

| Cost Category | Annual Cost |
|--------------|------------|
| Caseworker payroll (320 FTEs × $81K avg) | $25.9M |
| Administrative overhead (data entry, form processing, coordination) | $8.0M |
| Unclaimed benefits (40% of eligible residents don't claim) | $216M lost to residents |
| Avoidable crisis response (emergency shelter, hospitalization, family services) | $45.0M |
| **Total Annual Cost / Burden** | **$294.9M** |

**Root cause**: No unified intake system. Residents give up. Caseworkers manually re-enter data across 8–12 agency portals. Crisis moments go undetected.

---

### With HAVEN — Optimized Operations

**Efficiency Gains**:

| Improvement | Mechanism | Saving |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| 40% caseworker productivity gain | BB autofill, 1-click agency submission, automated tracking | 70 fewer FTEs needed |
| 70% admin overhead reduction | Automated form processing, integrated agency bridge | $5.6M/year |
| 80% application completion rate (vs. 40%) | Guided resident experience, BB assistance | $216M new benefits to residents |
| Crisis response time: 48hr → 4hr | BB crisis detection, real-time hotline routing | $30M avoided crisis costs |

**Net Savings Calculation (Year 1)**:

```
Caseworker Payroll Reduction       $5.67M
  (70 FTEs × $81K avg)
Administrative Overhead Reduction  $5.60M
  (70% of $8M)
Crisis Prevention Savings          $30.0M
  ($45M → $15M avoidable crisis costs)
HAVEN Platform Cost (Professional) -$0.18M
  ($15K/month × 12)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Net Annual Savings to County:      $41.1M

PLUS: $216M in new benefits flowing
      to residents who previously
      couldn't navigate the system.

Return on Investment (ROI):        ~230x
Cost per Resident Served:          $2–3/year
  (vs. $4,900 current per-capita spending)
```
