All NDAs include security commitments:

- [ ] Use strong passwords (16+ characters)
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication
- [ ] Keep credentials confidential
- [ ] Use approved SSO if available

### Data Handling
- [ ] Encrypt sensitive data
- [ ] Use secure communication (HTTPS, VPN, encrypted email)

- [ ] Don't share credentials

### Compliance
- [ ] Follow Project coding standards
- [ ] Pass security review before merge

- [ ] Comply with GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA
- [ ] Maintain access controls and monitoring

- [ ] Report security issues immediately to security@haven.local
- [ ] Don't publicly disclose vulnerabilities
- [ ] Cooperate with Company's response
- [ ] Provide timeline for remediation
- [ ] Follow up on fixes

## 🧑‍⚖️ LEGAL REVIEW RECOMMENDATIONS

### When to Seek Legal Counsel

**Review Before Signing If**:

- [ ] Contributing significant code or features
- [ ] Your organization contributes as a team
- [ ] You're contributing sensitive or proprietary information
- [ ] This is a formal partnership or sponsorship
- [ ] You have concerns about any terms
- [ ] You're not clear on any obligations
- [ ] There are special requirements or negotiations

### What a Lawyer Should Review

1. **Confidentiality Scope**
   - What information is protected?
   - Are there carve-outs you need?

2. **IP Ownership**
   - Who owns contributions?
   - What licenses apply?
   - Can you use your work elsewhere?

3. **Liability & Indemnification**
   - What are your exposure and obligations?
   - Are there caps on liability?

4. **Termination & Survival**
   - How long do obligations last?
   - What happens to your access?

5. **Dispute Resolution**
   - How are disagreements resolved?
   - Where would disputes go?

## 📞 SUPPORT & QUESTIONS

### Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to sign an NDA to contribute?**  
A: Yes, all contributors must sign an NDA to protect Haven's confidential information.

**Q: Which NDA should I use?**  
A: Use Short Form if you prefer simplicity, Full Template for comprehensive protection.

**Q: How long is the confidentiality obligation?**  
A: Typically 3-5 years after you stop contributing (specified in agreement).

**Q: Can I contribute code from my employer?**  
A: Only if your employer grants rights. Organizational NDA may be needed.

**Q: What if I find a security vulnerability?**  
A: Report to security@haven.local. Do NOT publicly disclose. See vulnerability disclosure section.

**Q: Can I modify the NDA?**  
A: Some terms are flexible. Contact legal@haven.local to discuss modifications.

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Typically 1-5 business days after signed NDA is submitted.
