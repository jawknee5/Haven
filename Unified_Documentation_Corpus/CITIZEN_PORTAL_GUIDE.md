## 📋 CREATING A CASE

### What's a Case?
A case is a formal request for help. Examples:
- Need housing assistance
- Looking for job training
- Need medical resources
- Food insecurity
- Other support needed

### Create New Case

#### Step 1: Start Case
1. From dashboard, click "Create New Case"
2. Or click "+" button

#### Step 2: Fill in Basic Info
```
Case Title:
[What do you need help with?]
Example: "Looking for affordable housing"

Category:
[Select one]
├─ Housing
├─ Food & Nutrition
├─ Employment & Job Training
├─ Medical & Health
├─ Education
├─ Transportation
├─ Utilities & Bills
└─ Other
```

#### Step 3: Provide Details
```
Tell us more:
[Detailed description of your situation]

What's your main concern?
├─ Need it immediately
├─ Within 1-2 weeks
├─ Within 1 month
└─ General information

Any other notes:
[Additional information]
```

#### Step 4: Attachments (Optional)
- Upload documents (PDF, images, etc.)
- Examples:
  - ID documents
  - Pay stubs
  - Medical records
  - Proof of address
- Files encrypted and secure

#### Step 5: Review and Submit
1. Review all information
2. Make sure everything looks good
3. Click "Submit Case"
4. Your case is created!

### What Happens Next
```
Case Created Successfully!

Your Case ID: C-2026-001
Status: New (Awaiting caseworker review)

Next Steps:
1. A caseworker will review your case within 24 hours
2. You'll receive an email when status changes
3. Chat with BB if you have questions
4. You can view your case anytime

You'll be notified when:
✓ Case is being reviewed
✓ Resources are assigned
✓ Your appointment is scheduled
✓ Any updates to your case
```

## 💬 CHAT WITH BB

### What's BB?
BB is an AI assistant that can help you 24/7.
- Answer questions
- Provide resources
- Help with forms
- Offer guidance
- Answer FAQs

### Start Chat
1. Click BB icon (bottom right)
2. Or go to http://localhost:3000/bb
3. Chat window opens

### Chat Examples

**You**: "What documents do I need for housing?"
**BB**: "Here are the documents typically needed:
- ID (driver's license or passport)
- Proof of income (recent pay stubs)
- Proof of address
- References (optional)

Would you like help with anything else?"

**You**: "How do I upload documents?"
**BB**: "To upload documents:
1. Go to your case
2. Click 'Documents' tab
3. Click 'Upload Document'
4. Select file from computer
5. Click 'Upload'

Your documents are encrypted and secure."

**You**: "What's the address of the shelter?"
**BB**: "The Downtown Emergency Shelter is located at:
123 Main Street
City, State 12345

Phone: (555) 123-4567
Hours: Open 24/7

Would you like directions?"
