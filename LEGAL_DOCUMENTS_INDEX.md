- Fill in all sections completely

- Receive countersigned copy

- Wait for approval (1-5 business days)
- Setup development environment
- Begin contributing

## 🔍 WHAT EACH NDA COVERS

### Confidentiality Protection

**All NDAs Include**:
- Definition of Confidential Information
- Obligation to keep information secret
- Limitation on use (for Project only)
- Security requirements
- Return/destruction upon termination
- Exclusions from confidentiality

**Coverage Includes**:
- [ ] Source code (published & unpublished)
- [ ] Security procedures & encryption
- [ ] Bugs & vulnerabilities
- [ ] User data & PII
- [ ] Business information
- [ ] Infrastructure & deployment
- [ ] Roadmaps & future features

---

### Intellectual Property Rights

**All NDAs Include**:
- Warranty that contributions are original
- License grant to Haven
- IP ownership clarification
- Third-party component handling
- Attribution preferences

**Options Available**:
- [ ] Retain copyright of contributions
- [ ] Assign copyright to Haven

---

### Security Obligations

**All NDAs Include**:
- Strong password requirements
- Two-factor authentication
- Secure storage practices
- Vulnerability disclosure process
- Incident reporting
- Data protection requirements
- Access control requirements

**Specific Requirements**:
- [ ] 16+ character passwords
- [ ] 2FA on all accounts
- [ ] VPN on public networks
- [ ] No personal device copies without permission
- [ ] Report incidents within 24 hours

---

### Vulnerability Disclosure

**All NDAs Include**:
1. Report to security@haven.local
2. DO NOT publicly disclose
3. Provide reproduction steps
4. Allow 30-90 days for fix
5. Do NOT exploit vulnerability
6. Eligible for bug bounty program

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I have to sign an NDA?**  
A: Yes, all contributors must sign an NDA to protect Haven's confidential information.

**Q: Which NDA should I sign?**  
A: See decision tree above. If unsure, contact legal@haven.local.

**Q: Can I modify the NDA?**  
A: Some terms are negotiable. Contact legal@haven.local to discuss.

**Q: How long does NDA approval take?**  
A: Typically 1-5 business days after submission.

**Q: What if I'm contributing from my employer?**  
A: Employer rights agreement and organizational NDA may be needed.

**Q: Is there a bug bounty program?**  
A: Yes, for security vulnerabilities reported per NDA terms.

**Q: How long is the confidentiality obligation?**  
A: Typically 3-5 years after you stop contributing (specified in your NDA).
