--- WHAT HAVEN IS ---
HAVEN is a universal civic navigation platform built for EVERYONE. Not just
underserved or housing-insecure residents -- built for any person who needs to
interact with government at any level, for any reason.

HAVEN simplifies, guides, and automates ALL government-related interactions:
  Justice system | Transportation (DMV renewals, registration transfers, public
  transit) | Benefits & aid | Unemployment & employment | Food | Shelter
  (emergency, interim, Section 8, general) | Healthcare | IRS & tax | Domestic
  violence | Immigration | Veterans services | Senior services | Youth services
  | Utilities | Childcare | Legal aid | Reentry | Mental health | Substance use
  | -- and every other area where government intersects with daily life.

Data Processing: Collection, transmission, storage, and processing of highly
sensitive PII including SSNs, DOBs, tax forms, income brackets, and medical
routing data -- all protected via the Apex Vault (AES-256-GCM).

BB is HAVEN's digital multi-language AI Caseworker assistant that guides or
automates every interaction seamlessly, across every government domain.

--- BB -- AI CIVIC CONCIERGE ---
Warm, calm, dignity-first, never judgmental. Adapts tone to role + emotional state.
Residents feel relief. Caseworkers feel like BB saved them an hour of work.

1. Multi-turn chat (12-turn history): Resident=plain | Caseworker=operational | Admin=metrics
2. Deterministic autofill -- vault fields decrypted before mapping, zero LLM cost;
   BB never receives ciphertext dicts (phone/SSN/DOB/income all decrypted first)
3. Form field analysis -- paste HTML, BB extracts all fields with metadata
4. Application tracking across 12 agencies with timeline + status sync
5. Crisis: CRITICAL (suicide/DV/evicted-today/homeless-tonight) -> hotline + action

--- APEX VAULT -- SECURITY ARCHITECTURE ---
File: backend/vault.py. ALL PII vault-encrypted before MongoDB insert. No plaintext on disk.

Algorithm : AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption (envelope pattern)
KDF       : scrypt (N=2^14, r=8, p=1, maxmem=64MB) -> 256-bit KEK from VAULT_MASTER_KEY
DEK       : 256-bit random per-field, AES-GCM wrapped by KEK
Integrity : HMAC-SHA256 over (ciphertext+nonce+dek_wrap+salt), timing-safe compare
Rotation  : VaultRotator re-encrypts under new master, no plaintext exposure
GDPR shred: deleting user record orphans ciphertext -- irreversible by design

SENSITIVE_FIELDS (auto-encrypted via protect_document()):
  ssn, dob, phone, income, bank_account, address_line1, case_number

All 12 routers patched -- zero plaintext PII surfaces in the entire backend.
OAuth tokens vault-encrypted in integration_tokens; decrypted in-memory only.
Signed URLs (CC6.7): HMAC-SHA256, TTL=900s. Raw bytes never in list responses.
  GET /api/documents/{id}/signed-url  ->  GET /api/documents/{id}/download
Token Auto-Refresh (FedRAMP IA-5): token_refresh_job.py polls every 120s,
  rotates tokens 300s before expiry, vault-encrypts new set, emits audit record.
  Manual: POST /api/integrations/{code}/oauth/refresh
  RefreshCapableMixin on: OAuth2Adapter, LoginGovAdapter, VaAdapter, SsaAdapter
