## 📋 KEY PROVISIONS IN ALL NDAs

### 1. Confidentiality Obligations
- Keep all disclosed information secret
- Limited use (Project only)
- Don't share without permission
- Return/destroy upon termination

### 2. Intellectual Property Rights
- Contributions are original work
- Contributor warrants no IP infringement
- License to Haven to use contributions
- Options to retain or assign copyright

### 3. Security Requirements
- Strong passwords & 2FA
- Secure storage & encryption
- VPN on public networks
- Report vulnerabilities safely
- Comply with data protection laws

### 4. Vulnerability Disclosure
- Report security issues to security@haven.local
- Do NOT publicly disclose
- Allow 30-90 days for fix
- Do NOT exploit vulnerabilities
- Eligible for bug bounty program

### 5. Termination & Survival
- Agreement term: 3-5 years after contribution ends
- Confidentiality survives indefinitely
- Return all materials upon termination
- IP rights continue after termination
